Instructions

The goal is to get every light bulb switched on at the same time.

The switches along the bottom will turn on or off a select group of the bulbs

By pressing a combination of these switches, it is possible to turn on every light, but extremely difficult

If you want to restart any level, please press the reset button in the menu

When you complete a level, your score is the number of switches you pressed to turn them on, to see your best score, take a look at your Stats!
